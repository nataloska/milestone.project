Ruben de Ronde is a Trance artist from the Netherlands, producing mainly Progressive Trance, both deep & electro influenced. He helps out Armin van Buuren with his weekly hit radio show A State of Trance, and also runs his own show called The Sound of Holland. Before producing music, he was one of the A&R people for Armada Music.
